Daisy Flower

The daisy flower is a simple yet beautiful plant known for its white petals and bright yellow center. It symbolizes purity, innocence, and new beginnings, and is commonly found in gardens and wild fields. Daisies are hardy flowers that can grow in many environments and are often associated with happiness and freshness.

Hibiscus Flower

The hibiscus, from the genus Hibiscus, is a colorful tropical flower valued for its beauty and uses. Species like Hibiscus rosa-sinensis are grown for decoration, while Hibiscus sabdariffa is used to make healthy herbal tea with medicinal benefits.
